Obliged entity list

To report a discrepancy you must be one of the following:

  • Credit Institutions
  • Financial Institutions
  • Auditors, external accountants and tax advisors
  • Notaries and other independent legal professionals
  • Estate agents including when acting as intermediaries
  • Other persons trading in goods in cash in an amount of EUR 10 000 or more
  • Providers of gambling services
