Report a discrepancy about a beneficial owner on the PSC register

Use this service if you are an obliged entity and the information you hold about a beneficial owner is different from the information on the people with significant control (PSC) register.

You'll need:

  • the company number for the PSC with the discrepancy
  • details of the discrepancy, for example an incorrect address or an invalid PSC statement
  • the date when the discrepancy was first noticed
  • the business address of the obliged entity making the report

Reporting a discrepancy takes up to 10 minutes.
