What a beneficial owner is

A beneficial owner is the person who benefits from a legal entity and exercises control over it and the assets it holds.

A beneficial owner does not have the same definition as a PSC. The requirement to report discrepancies is based on the Companies Act definition of a PSC.

Read how to identify PSCs of companies and limited liability partnerships (LLPs) and how to identify Scottish qualifying partnerships.
