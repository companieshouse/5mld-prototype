Make a discrepancy report

To report a discrepancy, you'll need:

  • the company number of the entity being reported as having a discrepancy
  • the type of discrepancy
  • what information on the PSC register is incorrect, for example an incorrect address or an invalid PSC statement
  • the correct information as you believe it should be shown on the register
  • any other relevant information about the discrepancy that supports the report

Reporting a discrepancy takes up to 10 minutes.

Before you start

Read our privacy statement before you make a report.

If one or more person with significant control (PSC) for a company has their details protected, you will not be able to use this service to report a discrepancy. You must contact the PSC discrepancy team directly instead.
